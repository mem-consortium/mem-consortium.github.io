About the Series

The Virtual Seminar Series provides a platform for presenting and discussing research papers, offering members the opportunity to share their work, receive feedback, and refine their research. These regular seminars foster collaboration and improve research quality through peer interaction.

To receive seminar announcements and Zoom links, please sign up as a member.
